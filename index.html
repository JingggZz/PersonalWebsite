About me

I am a Chinese illustrator who focus on visual communication. Not limited to painting, I like exploring new materials to make new kind of "illustration". Illustration is not just drawing, it can be everything and what the illustrator make can be illustration. I haven't stop exploring new skills, new materials and new way to show my thinking and my design.

Now I mainly focus on using computer programming to create interactive works. As I come to know about new medias and technology, I found that audiences are very imporatant part to art experiences. So my art experiment more focus on how audiences can interact with my works in order to explore the relationship between the technology and human.
